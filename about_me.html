About Me

a short story about my journey on the IT space

My name is Zuriel Joseph Jowy Mone, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Applied Information Technology at Politeknik Negeri Jakarta. My interest in technology began during high school, particularly in computer networking. I enjoyed learning how to design office networks, create network topologies, run simulations, and manage network infrastructure.

My interest in software development started when I was introduced to HTML in school. From that point, I became curious about how applications are built, why they are created, the technologies behind them, and the history of computers and software. This curiosity motivated me to explore web development and programming further.

After being accepted into Politeknik Negeri Jakarta, I expanded my knowledge of software engineering, including application design, development, maintenance, and team collaboration. During my studies, I was also introduced to machine learning and data analytics, which sparked a new interest in understanding how data can be used to predict outcomes, analyze trends, and segment users based on their behavior. Today, I continue to develop my skills in software development while exploring the potential of data-driven solutions to solve real-world problems.
